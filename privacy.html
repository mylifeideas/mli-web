Privacy Policy for Stock DCA

Last Updated: May 8, 2026

1. Introduction

My Life Ideas Ltd ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Stock DCA mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal data is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

Depending on how you use the app, we may process the following categories of information:

  • Data You Enter in the App: Information you choose to input, such as ticker symbols, shares, average cost, target shares, dividend values, and related DCA entries.
  • Support Communication Data: If you contact us by email, we receive your email address and any information you include in your message.
  • Limited Technical Data: Basic technical details may be included by your device or email client when you contact support.

We do not currently provide in-app account creation or login, and we do not intentionally collect account credentials through the app.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use information for the following purposes:

  • To provide core app functionality, including storing and displaying your portfolio-tracking data.
  • To respond to support or feedback requests.
  • To maintain app reliability and improve features over time.

4. Data Storage, Sharing, and Disclosure

Data you enter into the app is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We may share information only in limited circumstances:

  • System Integrations Initiated by You: If you choose to use device-level features (for example, sending feedback via your email app), relevant information is handled by that external service under its own terms and privacy policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Security

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information.

6. Your Choices and Data Rights

Because most data is stored locally on your device, you can update or remove entered data directly in the app. You can also remove app data by uninstalling the app, subject to your device platform behavior. If you contact us and have data-rights questions, we will do our best to assist in accordance with applicable law.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Third-Party Links and Services

The app may open external links (such as legal pages or support email). Third-party websites and services operate under their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their practices.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
